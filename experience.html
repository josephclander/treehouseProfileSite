Teaching

As a teacher I have had lots of experience of trying to create something that is best for the user to understand. The best lessons are formed from activities where the student can play with the ideas and figure out how concepts work for themselves.

Also as Deputy Head of department of Maths, I have held a lot of responsibilities; keeping a team working well together, training new staff in new practices and keeping check that we are all providing the same high standard of content.

The background design you see on the home page was created by one of my younger classes. They were exploring the relevance of the code that goes into old games and how Maths has improved the gameplay and graphics.

Web Development

I have worked on the up keep of a small website but wanted to learn how to push out more materials to the students. I started making a few screencasts, which turned into a Youtube page and now learning to build my own websites to host these materials the way I want to.

My experience to date includes completing HTML/CSS courses with Free Code Camp, General Assembly and now working through Tree House's Techdegree.

With a degree in Mathematics and an A-Level in French, I have gained an interest in Javascript. I have learned much of the syntax and when I become proficient in frontend design, I wish to take this further to add interactivity and explore the possibilities of backend development.
